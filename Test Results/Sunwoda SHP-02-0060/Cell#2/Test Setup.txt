This was a single charge cycle test at 11C, to invalidate DonutLab's claim that no NMC lithium cell could handle 11C charging from empty to 80% without active cooling.

Test results:
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=unQrgIvD5Xg

After this charge test completed, the cell rested for one hour, and was then discharged at 1C (6 A) back down to the starting voltage (3.0 volts).  During this discharge, the cell sourced 5.79 Ah.  Thus, the round trip Coulomb efficiency was 99.
